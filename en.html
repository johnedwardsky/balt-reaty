Popular Locations

Zelenogradsk

The resort capital. Endless promenade, cozy European streets, and best infrastructure for rest. Perfect for investment and year-round rental.

Svetlogorsk

A quiet harbor among pines. "Russian Switzerland" with hilly landscape and old German villas. Choice for those who value privacy and purest air.

Kaliningrad

The heart of the region. Fishing Village, Kant Island, and prestigious Amalienau districts. Comfort of a modern city combined with historical charm.

Buying Real Estate in Kaliningrad and the Region

If you plan to buy an apartment in Kaliningrad or acquire a private house on the coast (Zelenogradsk, Svetlogorsk, Baltiysk, Yantarnyy), it is important to choose a reliable partner. The regional real estate market offers both apartments in German houses with history and modern new developments.

Property prices depend on the area. Apartments in Selma, Central district, and near Upper Lake are popular. For those wishing to live by the sea, we offer a selection: buy a house in Svetlogorsk or apartments in Zelenogradsk.

We work with objects that are not on popular classifieds, check legal purity, and help process the transaction safely. The database includes: resales, houses under construction, land plots, and commercial real estate.
